All Rounder Squad

Before the youtube channel, it all started in the ninth grade when my obsession for listening to songs sky rocketed. I loved how music could just elevate your emotions like it was witchcraft except the fact that it actually existed and people make it. I wanted to learn how to produce music like the greats in the music industry such as Skrillex. I saw a youtube video in which I came to know of the software using which I could create professional music. I started learning it slowly. In my building, two of my friends too were interested in creating music. So we three met up and posted a few songs together. We did not gain too much traction. But I was pretty impressed with what I had made and how many views it had gained online. We continued creating and posting media slowly over time until we got tangled in our educations and went our separate ways.

Now, this channel is on hold as all three of the members live very far away and don't talk much. But who knows, maybe someday in the distant future we could come together again and make something new again. But at the moment, I post media anonymously online to see what type of media pleases the social media's algorithm.
