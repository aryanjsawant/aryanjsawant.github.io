Profile Website

We had a subject called web programming in our second semester and were taught basic html,css and js. I for one thought that it was pretty easy to make a website and anyone could do it. Because of this and the cumbersome testing I was not very fond of this. All my other friends were making pretty cool websites and I felt a bit left out.

My end semester exams concluded and left with me a with a very big summer vacation and i thought I could use it to build unique projects. I like making things of my own which struck me with an idea to create a website. I made a rough sketch of what the website should look like and how it would function.Since i had seen many profile websites made by many web designers i decided to create one of my own

As I had previously thought, making a website would be very easy. when I started creating, initially nothing was working as I wanted it to and it was very very frustrating.Since I hadn't studied web programming in very detail, I was facing many issues in between. But I kept on trying and things started working out. When i used to get stuck, I would just see a youtube video on it and move on. As I kept coding the website, I saw new new opportunities to improve my website and these detours were very costly, timewise. Nevertheless, it took me 2-3 weeks to create and implement such a simple design.

Surprisingly, the hardest part for me was choosing the color scheme, for which I searched up on youtube and found out about the 60-30-10 rule, which was very helpful. Finally I bought the cheapest domain on my name and fortunately it was my most favourite one.
